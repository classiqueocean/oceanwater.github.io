Chouyan Apparel LLC, pronounced (show-yan) was established in 2017 by Ocean Gordon. Chouyan Apparel LLC is dedicated to providing unique hand crafted affordable street wear. From the sketch book to the clothing, all designs are hand drawn and tell their own stories from the intricute little details to the big and bold colors, here at Chouyan we believe in our motto "draw what you feel, then wear it".

Brand Collections & Projects

All designs are hand drawn first then transferred over to Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator to be edited and finanlized.
