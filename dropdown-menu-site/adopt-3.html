So you’re looking to adopt a Ferret? We’re glad you’ve come to the right place to find out about Ferret adoptions. Ferrets are a lot like small dogs or cats, they require several hours of exercise per day, and can be trained with a litter box. Here are the personality descriptions of three ferrets for adoption that were once listed on our website. Meet, Trixie one most the beautiful ferrets we have seen in some time, with a little pink nose, luscious coat and lovely disposition. We are always amazed to see how eager Trixie is for affection and how engaging she is with our staff. Trixie , like a lot of other ferrets for adoption at our rescue, enjoys running through tubes, pouncing on crinkle sacks, and popping in peanuts!
