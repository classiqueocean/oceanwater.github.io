Date Caramel

Ingredients

  • 20 pitted Medjool dates.
  • 2/3 to 3/4 cup hot oat milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of seasalt, optional

Instructions

  1. Soak the dates Place pitted dates in a bowl and cover with hot Water

  2. Drain the dates After soaking, drain the dates of the Water
